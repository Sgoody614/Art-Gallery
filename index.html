About me

Hello and welcome! My name is Sean Goodrich, I'm 23 and I'm from Peoria, Arizona. I've been painting for just over a year now and I love it! My goal as an artist is to elicit the same feelings I get when I paint; happiness, serenity, and oneness.

How to purchase

  1. All inquiries are received using the email button at the bottom of the page
  2. Include the painting(s) ID number and your shipping address in the email
  3. I will send you a Paypal secured invoice via email
  4. All items will be shipped within 24 hours once payment has been received

    5. I am working on improving the purchasing process so I appreciate your patience in the meantime.
